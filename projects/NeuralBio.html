Biological to Artificial Neural Networks

  • Tech Stack: Ilastik, Python, Napari
  • Members: Me, José Geraldo De Carvalho

Developed a pipeline which collect the data from the Biological Neural Networks, to train and test the pixel classifier from Napari and Ilastik tools.

Choosed the application's architecture from Knime, to process each of the data generated from the National Biotechnology Laboratory.

The application developed with Ilastik and Napari package implemented with Python, that find the synapses and count each of the neurons.
